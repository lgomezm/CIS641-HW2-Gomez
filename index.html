My current project

I am working with Alex Billedeaux and Dan Dietsche on an exciting project!

We are implementing a rogue-like game. The main idea of the game is that the player walks through procedularly generated maps, while fighting enemies and picking up items. At the end of each map, there is an exit that takes the player to a new map. When the player reaches the exit, a new map with stronger enemies and new items will be generated. The player will continue on this loop until his/her character is defeated.
